Objectives:

- To develop a centralized and automated financial management system that consolidates revenues, expenses, budgets, transactions, and investments into a single platform.
- To reduce errors and inconsistencies caused by manual data entry and the use of disconnected financial tools.
- To provide real-time access to accurate financial data, enabling timely and informed decision-making.
- To enhance data security by implementing user authentication and role-based access control.
- To facilitate easy data entry, editing, and reporting through an intuitive and user-friendly interface.
- To support multi-user collaboration, allowing different departments or teams to manage financial data cohesively.
- To enable exporting of financial reports and data for compliance, auditing, and further analysis.
- To design the system with scalability and maintainability in mind, allowing future enhancements and integration with other enterprise systems.
